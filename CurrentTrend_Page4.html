A decreasing trend Summary below:

Since 1970, the implementation of the Clean Air Act and technological advancements by American innovators have significantly enhanced air quality in the U.S. During this period, combined emissions of criteria and precursor pollutants have decreased by 78%. Cleaner air brings substantial public health advantages, and we acknowledge our state, local, and industry partners for their contributions towards continuous long-term improvement in our air quality.

Air Quality Trends Show Clean Air Progress

  • Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2) Annual, 61%
